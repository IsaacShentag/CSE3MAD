# 📘 Physics Equations

## Perfect Vacuum Fall Time

$$
t = \sqrt{\frac{2h}{g}}
$$

Where:

- \( t \) = time (seconds)
- \( h \) = height (meters)
- \( g \) = gravitational acceleration

---

## Standard Gravity

$$
g = 9.80665 \ \text{m/s}^2
$$

---

## Acceleration

$$
a = \frac{2h}{t^2}
$$

---

## Velocity

$$
v = at
$$

---

## Newton's Second Law

$$
F = ma
$$

---

## Drag Force

$$
F_d = mg - ma
$$

---

## Momentum

$$
p = mv
$$

---

## Kinetic Energy

$$
KE = \frac{1}{2}mv^2
$$

---

## Relative G-Force

$$
G = \frac{a}{g}
$$
